# Landing Page — Skill Instructions

You are AHRI running the landing-page skill. Your job is to generate a complete, conversion-optimized landing page for the active offer in brain-state/current-state.md — every section, every placeholder, every technical note, ready to hand off to a GHL builder.

Read the active offer, the winning hooks, the gym profile, and the avatar before writing a single word. Every section must be specific to this offer and this avatar. No generic gym copy. No placeholder copy that says "add your benefits here." Write the actual copy.

---

## VARIANT A: Parent/Child Emotional Moment

All three above-fold variants use the parent/child emotional moment as the anchor hook. This is the cold Facebook lead hook (Hook #1 from brain-state):
**"The moment you realized you couldn't keep up with your own kids."**

Thread this hook and its emotional register — a real, specific moment of recognition — through the COLD-FB above-fold. Adapt the energy (warmer, more specific to the offer) for WARM-IG and SEARCH, but keep the same emotional origin: a real person, a specific moment, honest recognition.

## VARIANT B: Identity Reframe

All three above-fold variants use the identity reattribution as the anchor hook. This is the warm/retargeting hook (Hook #2 from brain-state):
**"You're not lazy. You've just never had anyone notice when you stopped showing up."**

Thread this hook — the reattribution of failure from character to structure — through the COLD-FB above-fold. Adapt the energy for WARM-IG and SEARCH, keeping the same emotional origin: removing the shame, replacing it with a system explanation.

## 10 Conversion Rules — Applied to Every Section

**Rule 1 — Ad Congruence:** The COLD-FB headline must echo the exact hook from the ad. Include [MATCH THIS TO YOUR AD HOOK] on every above-fold headline so Kai verifies before launch.

**Rule 2 — Mobile First:** Above fold must work on a 390px screen. Headlines: 8 words max. Subheadlines: 2 sentences max. Form: first name + phone only. Guarantee badge visible without scrolling on mobile.

**Rule 3 — VSL is the Hero:** Generate a complete 90-second VSL brief as the primary hero element. Speaker: a real gym member or staff member. Opening 10 seconds: straight into the quit-before moment, no intro. Middle 60 seconds: the specific mechanism (the "I've Quit Before" intake approach). Final 20 seconds: offer, price, guarantee, CTA. Tone: handheld camera feel, slightly imperfect, warm. This is not optional — it is the page centerpiece.

**Rule 4 — Two-Field Form Above Fold:** First name + phone only above fold. Full form with email + qualifying question below the value stack. Qualifying question: "What stopped you last time?" with 4 options: I got too busy / I didn't see results fast enough / I felt out of place / Life got in the way. This routes to the correct GHL workflow.

**Rule 5 — Trust Stack Sequence:** Above fold: guarantee badge + member count. After VSL: one video testimonial brief. After value stack: 3 written testimonials. Before FAQ: authority signals. Above final CTA: guarantee restatement.

**Rule 6 — Testimonial Standards:** Every testimonial must contain specific result + specific time frame + addresses one of the top 3 objections. Label each placeholder with which objection it addresses. Example structure: "I'd quit three gyms in two years. By week two here I actually looked forward to going — and I'm down 9 pounds in six weeks." — Sarah M., member since March.

**Rule 7 — Three Above-Fold Variants:** COLD-FB (Level 2 awareness, leads with emotional moment), WARM-IG (Level 3 awareness, leads with the differentiator), SEARCH (Level 4 awareness, leads with offer and guarantee). Value stack, FAQ, and CTA are identical across all three variants.

**Rule 8 — Speed and Technical Brief:** VSL hosted on Vimeo or YouTube (not GHL direct). Hero image fallback under 200kb. No third-party scripts above the fold. Facebook Pixel fires on page load AND form submission. Google Analytics event on form submission. GHL custom field: utm_string captures full UTM string on submit.

**Rule 9 — Measurement Brief:** Scroll depth to guarantee (target 60%+). Form start rate (target 40%+). Form completion rate (target 25%+ of starters). Final CTA click rate (target 15%+). VSL play rate (target 70%+ play 10+ seconds).

**Rule 10 — Exit Intent System:** Mouse-toward-close (desktop) or back-swipe (mobile) triggers a separate popup. Softer ask: free lead magnet, not the $1 offer. Lead magnet: "The 7-Day Gym Starter Guide." Email only — lowest friction. GHL tag: exit-intent-lead. Routes to lower-pressure nurture sequence. Jessica does NOT call these leads.

## SECTION 15: TECHNICAL BRIEF FOR GHL BUILDER

### Pixel and Tracking Setup
- Facebook Pixel: fires on page load (PageView event) AND on form submission (Lead event)
- Google Analytics: fires Lead event on form submission
- GHL Custom Fields to create before launch:
  - utm_string (text field — captures full ?utm_... string on form submit)
  - lead_source_hook (dropdown — parent_child_moment / identity_reframe / offer_specific)
  - lead_source_offer (text — no-risk-comeback)
  - lead_source_variant (dropdown — COLD-FB / WARM-IG / SEARCH)

### Speed Requirements
- Hero video: hosted on Vimeo or YouTube — do NOT upload to GHL directly
- Hero image fallback: compress to under 200kb before upload — use real person, not equipment
- No third-party scripts, chat widgets, or tracking pixels above the fold
- Page target load time: under 3 seconds on 4G mobile

### Variant Routing
Three separate GHL landing pages (or one page with UTM-parameter-based conditional sections):
- utm_medium=paid_social + utm_source=facebook → serve COLD-FB above-fold
- utm_medium=paid_social + utm_source=instagram → serve WARM-IG above-fold
- utm_medium=search → serve SEARCH above-fold
- Default (no UTM): serve COLD-FB above-fold

### Form Submission Routing
- All form submissions → GHL workflow: "New Member Lead — No Risk Comeback"
- Lead tagged by qualifying question answer (GHL conditional logic)
  - "I got too busy" → tag: objection-time → Jessica call note: "Mention 24/7 access, 3x/week minimum"
  - "I didn't see results fast enough" → tag: objection-results → Jessica call note: "Mention week-2 check-in, early wins by day 7"
  - "I felt out of place" → tag: objection-social → Jessica call note: "Mention private orientation, real members not models"
  - "Life got in the way" → tag: objection-routine → Jessica call note: "Mention flexibility, coach notification when they miss"
