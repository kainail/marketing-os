# The 30-Day Kickstart — Your First Month Fully Coached, Just $1
## Feel like someone who actually goes to the gym — in 30 days, no experience required, no commitment until you've seen it for yourself.

---

### A/B Variant Angle

Variant A leads with the dream outcome: identity shift. The lifestyle member doesn't want a gym membership — they want to become someone who goes. Someone who doesn't have to argue with themselves every morning. Someone who just... goes. This version of the offer frames every component around that positive destination. The value stack is built to make the prospect see themselves 30 days from now — more energy, a habit that's forming, a place where someone knows their name — and feel that version of themselves is not only possible but structurally inevitable given what's included. The emotional center of this offer is not "try our gym." It is "become the person you've been meaning to become, and here is the specific scaffolding that makes it happen this time."

### The Guarantee

**Guarantee Option A — Outcome Guarantee:**
Complete 12 sessions in your first 30 days. If you don't feel noticeably stronger, more energetic, and more confident in the gym than you do right now — we'll refund every dollar. No questions. No awkward conversation. No fine print. We're betting on the program because we've seen it work too many times to hedge.

**Guarantee Option B — Effort Guarantee:**
Show up 12 times in 30 days. That's 3 sessions a week. If you do that and honestly feel like this wasn't worth it — full refund of your membership and you keep the workout plan, the meal guide, and the express library. You walked in, you did the work, and if we didn't deliver, you owe us nothing.

**Recommendation:** Guarantee Option B is stronger for the lifestyle member. Here's why: the lifestyle member's deepest fear is not "what if the gym doesn't work" — it's "what if I don't follow through." Option B is structured around their effort, which does two things simultaneously. First, it signals that the gym is confident enough in its system to guarantee the result conditional only on attendance — not on genetics, not on diet perfection, just on showing up. Second, the 12-session requirement functions as a commitment device (Cialdini: Consistency). The prospect who agrees to 12 sessions has made a specific, measurable commitment that their identity will work to fulfill. The guarantee doesn't just remove risk — it creates the behavioral structure that produces the result. And the "you keep everything" clause makes the worst-case scenario feel generous rather than punitive.

### Deployment Notes

**One-sentence offer summary:** We help people who've tried gyms and quit become someone who actually goes — in 30 days, fully coached, for $1 — with a money-back guarantee if 12 sessions don't change how you feel.

**Recommended awareness level for paid ads:** Level 5 (Most Aware) for retargeting audiences who have visited the website, engaged with previous content, or opened a lead form. Level 4 (Product Aware) for warm audiences who have engaged with social content but not yet visited the site. For cold audiences (Level 1–3), do not lead with this offer — lead with awareness-level content and nurture into the offer.

**Avatar segments this offer is strongest for:** Primary — The Lifestyle Member, age 35–50, who has had at least one previous gym membership that lapsed. Secondary strength with the "never been a gym person" sub-segment of the lifestyle member, because the private orientation and first-week guide specifically eliminate the inexperience barrier. Weakest for prospects under 30 who have no gym history — they don't carry the "quit before" wound this offer is architected to heal.
