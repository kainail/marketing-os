3. You're expecting a thirty-day physical transformation. Results happen. People feel different fast. But if your goal is dramatic physical change in one month, you'll be disappointed by any gym on earth that tells you that's realistic. We won't.

|---|---|---|---|
| Scroll depth to guarantee section | Hotjar scroll map OR GHL analytics scroll tracking | 60%+ of page visitors reach Section 5 | Rewrite above-fold or shorten VSL — not enough is pulling visitors past the fold |
| Form start rate (first field interaction — above-fold form) | GHL form analytics | 40%+ of all page visitors | Rewrite CTA button copy or guarantee badge — the above-fold ask is not landing |
| Form completion rate (submitted vs started — above-fold form) | GHL form analytics | 25%+ of form starters complete submission | Simplify form — check if phone number field is creating friction on mobile; test name-only above fold |
| Full form completion rate (below fold) | GHL form analytics | 60%+ of visitors who reach the full form | Rewrite form headline or qualifying question framing — friction is killing completions |
| Final CTA click rate | GHL click tracking on Section 12 button | 15%+ of all page visitors | Rewrite final CTA section — offer restatement or urgency line is not converting |
| VSL play rate | Vimeo or YouTube analytics — 10+ second views | 70%+ of visitors who see the VSL play 10+ seconds | Rewrite VSL
