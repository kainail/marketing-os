## SECTION 11: FAQ

**Section headline:** Honest answers to the questions most people don't ask out loud.

---

**Q: I've quit gyms before. What actually makes this different?**
A: Probably the check-ins. Every new member gets two real texts from a coach each week during their first 30 days — not automated messages, not a newsletter. If you go quiet, someone notices and reaches out. Most gyms process your payment and hope you show up. We built a system around the assumption that the first 30 days are hard, because we know that's when people stop.

---

**Q: What if I'm not in good enough shape to start? I don't want to feel embarrassed.**
A: This is the most common thing people tell us when they walk in, and it is also the most common thing people laugh about six weeks later. Your orientation session happens before your first real workout — you walk in knowing what to expect, what to use, and who to ask. The members here are real people at every level. Nobody is watching you. The only people who feel out of place are people who've never been inside and imagined it was full of people who look like they live at a gym. It isn't.

---

**Q: I have a weird schedule. I can't commit to specific days and times.**
A: The gym is open 24 hours a day, every day including holidays. There are no class times to hit, no windows where the gym is "available." You go when it works for your week — 5am, 11pm, Sunday, Christmas. Your coach builds your program around your actual schedule, not an ideal one.

---

**Q: What does the guarantee actually mean? What's the fine print?**
A: Complete 12 sessions in 30 days. Use the program your coach builds for you. If at the end of that 30 days you don't feel meaningfully better — more energy, stronger, glad you started — you ask for your money back and we give it to you. That's it. There is no fine print. The only condition is that you actually participate in the program, because the guarantee is based on the program working — and it works when it's followed.

---

**Q: What happens after the 30 days?**
A: You decide. There's no automatic rollover into a long-term contract without your knowledge. At the end of your first month, your coach will sit down with you, talk through what worked, and walk you through your options. If you want to continue — and most people do — you choose the membership structure that fits your life. If you don't, you don't. There's no pressure and no penalty.
